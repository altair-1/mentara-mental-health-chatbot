About Mentara

Mental disorders are usually treated by pharmacotherapy or psychotherapy. However, there is a global shortage of human resources for delivering such mental health services. In developed countries there are nine psychiatrists per 100,000 people available, while in developing countries there is one psychiatrist per ten million people.

To address this problem, conversational agents have arisen interest in recent years, particularly in psychoeducation, behaviour change and self-help. Mentara is a rasa based conversational chatbot developed with help of cognitive behavioural therapy with a view of helping people tackle their daily mental issues. Mentara helps users deal with issues such as anxiety, depression, getting rid of sad thoughts, and more.

Mentara uses natural language understanding to converse with users. It also maintains a journal to track the progress of users' conditions. Mentara also provides comfort and suggestions for dealing with the issues they are facing.
